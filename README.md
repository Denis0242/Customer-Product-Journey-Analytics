🚀 Executive Summary

Customer Product Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end Product Data Science project that analyzes the full customer lifecycle — from discovery and conversion to retention, profitability, and churn prediction.

The platform integrates customer analytics, product performance, unit economics, growth metrics, and predictive insights into a single interactive analytics system built using Python, Streamlit, Pandas, and Plotly.

This project demonstrates how data science and product analytics transform operational data into actionable business intelligence for product, growth, and strategy teams.

📈 Business Impact

This analytics platform enables product and growth teams to:

Identify high-value customer segments using lifecycle analysis

Detect customers at risk of churn before revenue is lost

Analyze product profitability and category performance

Monitor acquisition efficiency using CAC and LTV metrics

Diagnose conversion funnel drop-offs in the customer journey

Support data-driven product and marketing decisions

The system transforms raw operational data into actionable insights that improve revenue, retention, and product strategy.

📊 Dashboard Modules

The Streamlit dashboard contains 7 analytical modules designed to answer critical business questions.

1️⃣ Executive Overview

High-level business performance metrics including:

Total revenue

Total customers

Active customers

Average order value (AOV)

Repeat purchase rate

Gross margin

Purpose:
Quick snapshot of overall business health and performance.

2️⃣ Customer Journey Analysis

Customer funnel analysis across digital touchpoints.

Page View → Product View → Add to Cart → Checkout → Purchase

Insights include:

Funnel conversion rates

Stage drop-off analysis

Customer behavioral patterns

Purpose:
Identify friction points in the purchase journey.

3️⃣ Product Performance Analysis

Product catalog evaluation including:

Revenue by category

Top-performing products

Product lifecycle stages

Category profitability

Purpose:
Identify which products drive revenue and margins.

4️⃣ Unit Economics

Financial sustainability analysis including:

Revenue vs profit

Gross margin

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

LTV:CAC ratio

Purpose:
Evaluate whether growth is profitable and scalable.

5️⃣ Growth Metrics

Business expansion analysis including:

Month-over-month revenue growth

Customer acquisition trends

Cohort retention analysis

Payback period

Purpose:
Measure growth efficiency and retention performance.

6️⃣ Customer Experience (3D Analysis)

Multi-dimensional customer behavior visualization using:

Engagement

Purchases

Total spend

Purpose:
Understand high-value vs low-value customer segments.

7️⃣ Predictive Analytics

Forward-looking insights including:

Customer Lifetime Value estimation

Churn risk prediction

High-value customer identification

Purpose:
Move from descriptive analytics to predictive decision support.
