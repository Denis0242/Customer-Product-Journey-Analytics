# 📊 Customer Product Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end product analytics project built with Python, Streamlit, and Plotly to analyze the full customer lifecycle—from acquisition and conversion to retention, unit economics, and churn risk.

---

## 📌 Overview

This project demonstrates how product and customer data can be transformed into actionable insights for product, growth, and business teams. It combines exploratory data analysis, KPI development, funnel analysis, cohort retention, unit economics, and predictive analytics in one interactive dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Product and growth teams need a clear view of how customers move through the journey, where they drop off, which segments create the most value, and whether growth is efficient.

Without structured analytics:

- Funnel drop-offs remain hidden
- Retention problems are harder to diagnose
- High-value customer segments are missed
- Growth may look strong while unit economics are weak
- Product and marketing decisions rely on assumptions instead of evidence

This dashboard solves that by turning raw customer and transaction data into business-ready insights.

---

## 🧩 Dashboard Modules

### 1. Executive Overview
Tracks high-level business performance, including revenue, active customers, average order value, repeat purchase rate, and margin.

### 2. Customer Journey Analysis
Measures movement through the funnel from page view to purchase and highlights drop-off points.

### 3. Product Performance Analysis
Shows which products and categories drive revenue, profitability, and long-term value.

### 4. Unit Economics
Evaluates whether growth is efficient using CAC, LTV, gross margin, and LTV:CAC ratio.

### 5. Growth Metrics
Analyzes customer acquisition, revenue trends, retention, and cohort performance over time.

### 6. Customer Experience Analysis
Explores engagement, purchase behavior, and spend patterns to identify high-value and low-value segments.

### 7. Predictive Analytics
Estimates churn risk and customer lifetime value to support proactive product and retention strategies.
