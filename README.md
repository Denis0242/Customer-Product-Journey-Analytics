## 📌 Executive Summary
End-to-end product analytics dashboard analyzing user lifecycle, funnel conversion, and engagement metrics to drive product decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Product teams need visibility into:
- User journey
- Funnel drop-offs
- Engagement and retention

---

## 📊 Dataset
- User events
- Sessions
- Transactions
- Lifecycle data

---

## 📈 Key Metrics
- DAU / MAU
- Conversion rate
- Funnel drop-off rates
- Retention

---

## 🔍 Analysis
- Funnel analysis (Login → Activation → Conversion)
- Cohort retention analysis
- Engagement tracking

---

## 💡 Insights
- Major drop-off at activation stage
- High engagement among retained users
- Conversion bottleneck identified

---

## ✅ Recommendations
- Simplify onboarding flow
- Improve activation experience

---

## 🎯 Decision
- Redesign onboarding and test improvements via A/B testing

---

## 💰 Business Impact
- Expected increase in activation and retention rates
